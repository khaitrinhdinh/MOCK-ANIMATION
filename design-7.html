Cafe in the RainYama

Asset-based pipeline

  • Progress-driven crossfade — four consistent growth scenes dissolve into each other as the loading percentage climbs, so the plant visibly grows in time with the real work.
  • Cinematic camera — a slow Ken Burns push-in runs throughout, with a stronger lean on the leaf near the end.
  • Warm dust + finale drop — golden motes drift up; at completion a golden drop falls and a ripple spreads over the final scene.
  • Light & simple — plain JPG scenes (~300KB total) + two small PNG overlays; pure CSS crossfade + GSAP, no WebGL.
Full-scene crossfade. The 4 AI scenes are baked backgrounds (not cut-outs), so this uses them as film frames that dissolve into each other — no transparency needed.

Swap in higher-resolution sprites and this exact scene becomes crisp — that is the whole point of the asset pipeline: the render code stays put, the quality rides on the art.
