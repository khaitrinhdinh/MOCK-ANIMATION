How it's built

  • Level-3 GPU pipeline — the illustration stays crisp SVG; behind it a 2D canvas drifts fog, dust and stars; in front a PixiJS WebGL layer renders the particle engine, glow cores and volumetric rays through a real AdvancedBloomFilter — actual bloom, not box-shadow.
  • Energy streams & fragments — thin bezier paths with a travelling dash (stroke-dashoffset loop) flow into the seed, while fine dots converge along their own --tx/--ty vectors and a dotted ring orbits the seed.
  • Layered growth — silhouette leaves behind, main stem + two side stems, four vein-detailed leaves, two tip leaves and buds, each on its own delay.
  • Triple-layer lotus — 6 + 6 + 4 translucent petals unfold, finished with a dotted halo ring around the glowing core.
  • Drop from the petals — the drop is anchored at the lotus centre and falls 140px into the pool.
  • Sound waves — besides the expanding ripples: pulsing arc waves on both sides and an 11-bar equalizer that starts dancing when the drop lands.
