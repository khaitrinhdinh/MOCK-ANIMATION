How it's built

  • Premium portrait footage — a coherent three-beat story: the Music Seed charges up, a golden sprout grows, then a dew drop falls and ripples. Each beat is its own <video>.
  • Shown whole, no video box — object-fit: contain keeps the full vertical composition, and mix-blend-mode: screen plus a soft edge mask dissolve the black background into the app.
  • Seamless phase switch — three stacked video layers cross-fade on opacity; nothing reloads, so there's no black flash between phases.
  • Compressed to 480p — the three clips together are ~0.9 MB (from 5.6 MB), audio stripped, indistinguishable at phone size.
  • UI is state-driven — phase label, level-coloured progress bar, status line and the checklist all follow the current phase; the backend just calls setPhase().
… total weight of this page as loaded — the three phase videos + UI, before gzip.

This demo plays each phase's clip through to the end, then hands off to the next. In the app, loop each clip and call setPhase(0/1/2) from the music-generation API's status — each phase runs until it's done.
