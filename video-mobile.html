Design 02 — Create Flow · Video Loader

How it's built

  • Real create-song flow — the LIFF wizard: seed → image → mood → vocal → text → confirm, with step dots, chat bubbles and inline options.
  • Fully mocked — sample seeds & moods, image kept as a local preview only. No upload, no Mureka call; runs on-hand data to test the UI.
  • Video processing screen — three real clips (Music Seed, sprout, dew drop) whose black background is dissolved into the app with mix-blend-mode: screen + an edge mask, crossfading across the three loading steps.
  • Compressed to 480p — the clips together are ~1.9 MB, indistinguishable at phone size.
